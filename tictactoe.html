Developed a Tic-Tac-Toe game using C, supports Multiplayer and Player vs AI. The AI leverages either a Minimax algorithm (perfect and imperfect) or a Naive Bayes model (Jarvis) trained on historical game data to predict outcomes and make strategic moves. The game also includes sound effects to enhance the player experience during gameplay.
